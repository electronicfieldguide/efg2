Identification: Large, forewing medial and subapical bands broken, paired white marginal spots below, strong hindwing discal bar, forewing rear margin all black.

Wingspan: 86

Forewing Length: 44 mm

Horizontal bars = 1cm


Local distribution: Above 600 m on Pacific slope; more common on Atlantic slope below 700 m.

Abundance: Uncommon
